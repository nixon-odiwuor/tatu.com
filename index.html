My Philosophy

I believe that every individual has the inherent capacity for growth and healing. My approach is rooted in creating a safe, non-judgmental space where clients can explore their thoughts and feelings freely.

Using evidence-based therapeutic techniques, I work collaboratively with my clients to develop personalized treatment plans that address their unique needs and goals.

Areas of Specialization

I offer comprehensive therapy services tailored to meet your individual needs and support your journey toward mental wellness.

Stress, Depression & Anxiety

Evidence-based treatments for managing stress, overcoming depression, and reducing anxiety through cognitive-behavioral therapy and mindfulness techniques.

Trauma & PTSD Therapy

Specialized trauma-informed care using EMDR and other proven methods to help process traumatic experiences and develop healthy coping mechanisms.

Grief & Loss Therapy

Compassionate support through the grieving process, helping you navigate loss and find meaning while honoring your loved one's memory.

Child & Adolescent Therapy

Age-appropriate therapeutic interventions for children and teens, addressing behavioral issues, emotional regulation, and developmental challenges.

Addiction Recovery

Comprehensive addiction treatment programs focusing on recovery, relapse prevention, and building sustainable healthy lifestyle patterns.

Family & Couples Therapy

Relationship counseling and family therapy to improve communication, resolve conflicts, and strengthen bonds between loved ones.

Psychological Assessments

Comprehensive psychological evaluations for diagnostic clarity, treatment planning, and understanding cognitive and emotional functioning.
